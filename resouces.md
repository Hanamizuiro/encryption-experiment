BSDS500 (best all-around academic benchmark)
500 natural images, common in image-processing papers
<https://www2.eecs.berkeley.edu/Research/Projects/CS/vision/grouping/segbench/>

Kodak Lossless Suite (classic baseline, small and clean)
24 high-quality images, widely used for compression/encryption tests
<https://r0k.us/graphics/kodak/index.html>

DIV2K (best for high-resolution stress tests)
2K-resolution images, good for runtime/scalability analysis
<https://data.vision.ee.ethz.ch/cvl/DIV2K/>

USC-SIPI (classic textures + misc images)
Great for matrix/numerical behavior tests
Main: <https://sipi.usc.edu/database/>
Copyright notes: <https://sipi.usc.edu/database/copyright.php>

CSIQ / LIVE (if you want pre-distorted/noisy images)
CSIQ: <https://s2.smu.edu/~eclarson/csiq.html>
LIVE info: <https://live.ece.utexas.edu/research/Quality/evaluation.htm>
