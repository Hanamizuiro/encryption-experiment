# Paper Figure Selection Guide

## Keep in main paper body
1. `out/figures/metrics_dense.png` OR a combined dense/sparse metric panel
2. `out/figures/gs_convergence_traces.png`
3. `out/figures/paper_triptych.png`
4. One representative error panel from `out/figures/wall_err_*.png`

## Move to appendix/supplement
- All remaining `wall_*.png`
- All remaining `wall_err_*.png`
- Expanded per-sigma tables from `out/tables/`

## Result tables to prioritize
- `out/results_solver_ranking.csv`
- `out/results_delta_vs_np_solve.csv`
- summary subset from `out/results_summary.csv`

## Claims alignment
- Use `docs/CLAIM_BOUNDARY.md` phrasing for limitation statement.
